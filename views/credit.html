Credit Tracking

CE Headquarters is designed to automatically record your continuing education credits. This unique system allows you convenient access to current medical information while attending to patients and simultaneously, your continuing education credit is recorded at the time of inquiry in time granules as small as seconds or minutes.

The site provides access to multiple accredited sources, and allows you to quickly address patient concerns with the most up-to-date information. In a matter of minutes, CE Headquarters enables you to provide the best medical care for your patients while reducing the administrative burden of tracking continuing education credits. The process simply requires that you indicate a request for credit by responding to three short inquiries.

Medical professionals may claim 0.5 AMA PRA Category 1 credit(s)TM for each search conducted. To receive credit, CE Headquarters makes it as easy as 1-2-3. At the time of the search simply complete the following questions:

  1. Describe the clinical question
  2. Cite the clinical sources
  3. Evaluate the application of your research to practice

Not all inquiries must be completed at the time of care. CE Headquarters allows you to save your search and return later to complete the questions in order to request credit.

Credit Designation

The University of Iowa Carver College of Medicine designates the completion of each CE Headquarters cycle for a maximum of 0.5 AMA PRA Category 1 credit(s)TM. You may only claim credit commensurate with the extent of your participation in the activity.

As specified by the AMA, a maximum 20 credits earned at the point of care can be claimed and attributed to receipt of AMA PRA Category 1 credit(s)TM certificates per year. Your credit certificates can be viewed and printed from CE Headquarters.
